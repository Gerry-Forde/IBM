## Abbreviation style

Do not use periods in all-uppercase abbreviations, such as SWAT or IBM.

I.B.M.

S.W.A.T.

## Commas

In a series of items connected by "and" or "or," retain the comma before the "and" and "or."

It comes in red, blue and green.
